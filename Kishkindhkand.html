Kishkindha Kand

Kishkindhakanda is a part (kanda or sopan) of the Ramayana by Valmiki and the Sri Rama Charit Manas by Goswami Tulsidas . Shri Ram came near Rishyamook mountain. Sugriva along with his ministers lived on that mountain . Sugriva, in the apprehension that Vali might not have sent the two heroes to kill him, sent Hanuman as a brahmin to inquire about Sri Rama and Lakshmana . After knowing that he had not been sent by Bali , Hanuman befriended Sri Rama and Sugriva . Sugriva consoled Shri Ram that Janaki ji would be found and he would help in finding her. as well as his brother BaliHe told about the atrocities committed against him. After killing Bali, Shri Ram gave the kingdom of Kishkindha to Sugriva and the position of crown prince to Angad , the son of Bali . Rama gives his ring to Maruti, so Sita can recognize him as a messenger After attaining the kingdom, Sugriva indulged in luxury and the rainy and autumn seasons passed. On Rama 's displeasure, Sugriva sent the monkeys to search for Sita . The monkeys who went in search of Sita saw an ascetic in a cave. Tapaswini took the search team to the beach with Yoga Shakti, where she met Sampati . Sampati told the monkeys that Ravana had kept Sita in the Ashokvatika of Lanka . Jambavan asked Hanuman to cross the ocean butHow will Hanuman ji go so far? Then Hanuman ji asked Jambwant ji that "How will I be able to go so far." Then Jambwant ji reminded him of his powers and told that he used to harass Shri Bhriguvanshi ji, due to which he got fed up and cursed Hanuman, "You forget your strength and brilliance forever but when someone remembers you your powers. Only then will you be able to use it."

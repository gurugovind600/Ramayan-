Yuddh Kand

The Lankakand is a part (kand or step) of Valmiki 's Ramayana and Goswami Tulsidas 's Shri Ram Charit Manas . On the orders of Jambavan , both Nal and Neel brothers built a bridge over the sea with the help of the monkey army. Shri Ram worshiped Lord Shankar after establishing Shri Rameshwar and went across the sea with the army. Ram camped across the ocean . Ravana was greatly disturbed by the news of the bridge being tied and Rama 's descent across the ocean . Ravana 's arrogance did not go away even after Mandodari persuaded him not to take enmity with Rama . Here Rama along with his Vanarasena started residing on the Subel mountain . Angad Ram As a messenger , he went to Ravana in Lanka and gave him a message to take refuge in Rama , but Ravana did not obey. When all efforts for peace failed, the war started. There was a fierce battle between Lakshmana and Meghnad . Lakshmana fainted from the blow of the power arrow. Hanuman brought Sushen Vaidya for his treatment and went to bring Sanjeevani . On receiving the news from the detective, Ravana sent Kalnemi to obstruct Hanuman 's work, which Hanuman killed. As the medicine was not recognized, Hanuman lifted the entire mountain and went back. Suspecting Hanuman to be a demon on the way , Bharat He fainted by shooting an arrow, but on knowing the truth, he was sent back to Lanka after sitting on his arrow. Seeing the delay in the arrival of the medicine, Rama started to babble. At the right time, Hanuman came with the medicine and Lakshmana became healthy due to Sushen 's treatment . Ravana woke up Kumbhakarna for the war . Kumbhakarna also gave an unsuccessful invitation to go to Rama 's refuge. In the battle, Kumbhakarna attained supreme speed at the hands of Rama . Lakshman fought with Meghnad and killed him. Many fierce battles took place between Rama and Ravana and in the end Ravana was killed by Rama . After handing over the kingdom of Lanka to Vibhishana , Rama along with Sita and Lakshmana climbed the Pushpakaviman to Ayodhya .

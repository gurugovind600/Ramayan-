Aranya Kand

Aranyakanda is a part (kanda or sopan ) of the Ramayana by Valmiki and the Sri Rama Charit Manas by Goswami Tulsidas . After some time Rama departed from Chitrakoot and he reached the ashram of Rishi Atri . Atri praised Rama and his wife Anasuya explained to Sita the meaning of Pativrat Dharma . From there again Rama went ahead and met Sharbhang Muni. Sharbhang Muni was residing there only with the desire to see Rama, so after fulfilling his desire to see Rama , he burnt his body with yoga fire and went to Brahmaloka . And on moving onHe saw heaps of bones at various places, about which the sages told Rama that the demons had eaten many sages and only those sages had bones. On this, Rama vowed that he would kill all the demons and make the earth demon - free. Rama went further and, meeting sages like Sutikshana , Agastya etc. on the way , entered the Dandak forest where he met Jatayu . Rama made Panchavati his abode. Sita Haran (painter: Ravi Varma) In Panchavati , Ravana 's sister Surpanakha came and made a courtship request to Rama . Rama sent him to Lakshmana , saying that he was with his wife and that his younger brother was alone . Lakshmana , rejecting his courtship, cut off his nose and ears, knowing the enemy's sister. Shurpanakha sought help from Khar- Dushan and he came to fight with his army. In the battle, Rama killed Khar-Dushan and his army. Shurpanakha went and complained to her brother Ravana . Ravana took Maricha to take revenge.Sent it as a golden deer , whose bark was demanded by Sita from Rama. By ordering Lakshmana to protect Sita , Rama went after her to kill Maricha , a golden deer . Maricha was killed at the hands of Rama , but while dying , Maricha cried 'O Lakshmana ' by making Rama 's voice , hearing which Sita , in apprehension, sent Lakshmana to Rama . After the departure of Lakshmana , Sita was abducted by Ravana deceitfully and accompanied her. taken to Lanka . On the way, Jatayu fought with Ravana to save Sita and Ravana cut off her wings and made her half dead. Rama was very sad on not finding Sita and started mourning. On meeting Jatayu on the way, he told Rama about his plight by Ravana and taking Sita to the south direction. After telling all this, Jatayu gave up his life and Rama after performing his last rites proceeded inside the dense forest in search of Sita . On the way, Rama killed Gandharva Kabandha , who became a demon due to the curse of Durvasa , and saved him. reached his ashram, where he ate the berries given by him under the control of his devotion. In this way, in search of Sita, Rama went ahead inside the dense forest.
